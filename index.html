Rockford, Il Web Devoplers

Rock the web

Rockford has web developers. Good ones, too. And they like to get together, talk about it and make things.

Who are we? We're web developers, devoplers and oplers. What do we do? Develop, devop, and op. When do we do it? At all reasonable times and some unreasonable times. Where do we do it? At home, at work and at hackathons. Why do we do it? Because it's fun and pays the bills. How do we do it? With text editors, IDEs, servers virtual and otherwise, and lots of staring into space.
