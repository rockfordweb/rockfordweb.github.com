About RockDevs

RockDevs is a group of software and web developers and other IT professionals in Rockford, Illinois, created with the goal of helping developers network, learn, and find employment. Rockford may not be known for its software industry - but there are more developers here than you might think.

  • Fourth Wednesdays: Northern Illinois Coders
